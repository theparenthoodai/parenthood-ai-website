a note from me

Welcome to Parenthood.AI

Hi and welcome to Parenthood.AI! I'm Hema. I'm a mom to two sweet girls, and I started parenthood.ai because I could see where things were going.

AI wasn't coming someday. It was already here. In the news, in my feed, in the tools my kids will grow up using. I had two choices. Be scared of it, or learn it.

I chose to learn it. And once I did, I couldn't unsee how much of the daily grind it could take off my plate.

That's what this is. A place to learn AI in plain words, whichever tool you want to use. No coding. No jargon. No pretending you've got a spare hour to figure it out.

Here's what it does for you. It simplifies the invisible work. The meal planning. The appointment juggling. The "what do I even make for dinner" spiral at 6pm with a toddler on your hip. You bring the tool you like. I'll show you how to make it actually help.

And it isn't only about getting through today. Our kids are growing up in a world where AI is everywhere. The classroom, the jobs they'll have that don't exist yet. Learning it now means we can guide them through it, instead of scrambling to catch up later.

You're a whole person, too. A career. A relationship. Money to sort out. A brain that used to hold more than snack schedules. Parenting is where we start. It isn't where we stop.

What's coming: free guides you can actually use, a blog for the longer stuff, and more tools built for real parent problems as this grows. Over on Instagram, the daily reminder that you're doing better than you think.

Come learn it with me.

Hema
